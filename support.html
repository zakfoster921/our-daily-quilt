Support

OUR DAILY QUILT is a collaborative daily quilt app by Zak Foster. If something is not working, first try closing and reopening the app, then make sure your device has an internet connection.

Common Questions

If notifications are not arriving, check that notifications are enabled for OUR DAILY QUILT in iOS Settings. If sharing or saving does not work, check that the app has permission to save to your photo library.

Quote suggestions are reviewed before they appear in the app. Submitting a quote does not guarantee it will be used.

Privacy and Data

You can reset your local color history from the app settings. To ask about data removal, quote submissions, or privacy, contact Zak Foster through zakfoster.com.

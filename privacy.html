Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

OUR DAILY QUILT is a small collaborative art app by Zak Foster. Each day, people read the same quote, choose a color, and those colors become a shared quilt.

Information We Collect

The app may create a random device identifier so your color can be placed in the daily quilt and so the app can remember your local color history. If you enter a first name, submit a quote, or choose to receive notifications, that information is sent to the services that run the app.

Submitted quote suggestions may include the quote text, author, and the name you provide. Quilt participation may include your selected color, the date, and app-generated identifiers needed to operate the shared quilt.

How We Use Information

We use this information to run the daily quilt, show your contribution, save quote suggestions for review, send optional notifications, prevent accidental data loss, and troubleshoot the app.

Service Providers

OUR DAILY QUILT uses trusted service providers, including Firebase/Google Cloud for app data and notifications and Railway for backend hosting. These services may process technical information such as device, browser, request, and crash or log data as needed to provide and secure the app.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Daily quilts and approved quotes may be visible to other app users or shared publicly as part of the project. Quote submissions are reviewed before they are used.

Your Choices

You can decline notifications, avoid entering a name, and reset your local color history from the app settings. For questions or removal requests, use the Support page.

Children

OUR DAILY QUILT is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The latest version will be posted here.
